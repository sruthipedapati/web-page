Discription About The Recipe

Cupcakes may be topped with frosting or other cake decorations. They may be filled with frosting, fruit, or pastry cream. For bakers making a small number of filled cupcakes, this is usually accomplished by using a spoon or knife to scoop a small hole in the top of the cupcake. Another method is to just insert the pastry bag in the middle of the cupcake. In commercial bakeries, the filling may be injected using a syringe. Elaborately decorated cupcakes may be made for special occasions.

Cupcakes are both delicious and adorable, which makes them perfect for wooing your loved ones.With the right cupcake recipe, you can even make baking them into a fun activity, especially with kids. You can play around with the frosting on the cupcakes and create some sweet stories on holiday.Our cupcake recipe is so simple, you won’t need to worry how to make cupcakes at home, and you can whip up a bunch in a jiffy. The result is a batch of these spongy cupcakes that can make a happy moment happier. Want to do something special for that special someone? This cupcake recipe will definitely do the job. They could even be your special anniversary or birthday cupcakes. So go on, give it a try and enjoy!


Ingredients

  1. Unsweetened cocoa powder for frosting
  2. cupMaida
  3. Baking soda
  4. Baking Powder
  5. Vanilla Essence
  6. Melted Butter
  7. Vanilla essence for frosting
  8. Powdered sugar for frosting
  9. Butter for frosting
  10. Nestle Milkmade

process

  • Sift Maida, baking soda and baking powder together. Whisk MILKMAID, butter and vanilla essence together. Fold this in the Maida mixtur
  • Preheat the oven to 150C. Pour the batter into moulds till 3/4th full and bake at 1500c for 20 minutes or till done. Cool and keep aside.
  • Beat ingredients for frosting together till evenly consistent and light. Fill piping bag with frosting and decorate on top
